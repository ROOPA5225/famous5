Kanaka Durga Temple

Kanaka Durga, also known as Kanaka Durga Devi or Sri Kanaka Durgamma, is the presiding deity of the famous Kanaka Durga Temple situated on the Indrakeeladri Hill in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. She is revered as one of the most powerful manifestations of the goddess Durga, embodying strength, protection, and compassion. This temple, located on the banks of the Krishna River, is an important pilgrimage site in South India and attracts thousands of devotees, especially during the annual Dasara festival.
