Murudeshwar Temple

The Murudeshwar Temple, located in the coastal town of Murudeshwar in Karnataka, India, is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is famous for its magnificent statue of Shiva and scenic surroundings. Nestled between the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats, this temple has become one of the most prominent Shiva temples in South India and is a popular pilgrimage and tourist destination.
