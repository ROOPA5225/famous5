Chamundeshwari Temple

The Chamundeshwari Temple, located on Chamundi Hill near Mysore in Karnataka, is a prominent Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Chamundeshwari, a powerful form of Durga also known as Mahishasura Mardini (slayer of the demon Mahishasura). The temple is not only a major pilgrimage site but also a symbol of Mysore’s rich cultural and spiritual heritage.
